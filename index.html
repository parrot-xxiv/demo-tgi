Our Commitment

Equipped with a vision and the right values, we are expanding and reaching new heights as a powerful, nurturing, and timeless conglomerate. We are moving towards a more promising future to cultivate opportunities and provide a customer experience like no other. Since 2001, Trends Group, Inc. (TGI) promises not only to give their business partners the satisfaction they deserve, but also aims to elevate itself to be part of an evolving society. TGI is ISO-certified. We were granted two certificates: (1) ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System, and (2) ISO/IEC 27001:2013 Information Security Management System. With these certifications, we can guarantee our partners and affiliates quality service that meets international standards.
